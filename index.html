About

I'm a Creative Lead and Senior Conceptual Copywriter at Amazon's in-house agency — awarded Global In-House Company of the Year 2023 at the London International Awards and Best In-House Agency 2025 at AdAge's A-List.

I lead global Prime and retail campaigns, working directly with the European ECD and directing external agencies, editors, and internal teams across the full production lifecycle — from originating concepts and writing scripts through to directing on-set, post-production, and social-native adaptations. I've built enduring creative platforms that have run across 12+ countries.

Outside of my day job, I direct short films and music videos and write screenplays — which means the craft of filmmaking isn't something I switch on at work. It's just how I think.

I'm also a mentor with the Creative Mentor Network, dedicated to fostering emerging talent from marginalised and underrepresented communities.
